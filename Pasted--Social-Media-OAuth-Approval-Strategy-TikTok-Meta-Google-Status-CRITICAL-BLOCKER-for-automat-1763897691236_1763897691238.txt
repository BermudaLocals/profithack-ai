2. Application Checklist and Best Practices

Item
Action
Rationale
App Name
Use a professional name like "ProfitHack AI Creator Suite" or "PHAI Management."
Avoid names that sound like spam bots (e.g., "AutoPoster," "GrowthHack").
Video Demo
CRITICAL: Provide a high-quality video demo showing a human user interacting with the app, not the automated posting running in the background.
Show the user selecting the AI-generated content, scheduling it, and reviewing analytics. This proves it's a "tool," not a "bot."
Privacy Policy
Must explicitly state how you use the posting permissions (e.g., "We use your granted permissions to post content to your account on your behalf and at your direction").
Transparency is key to approval.
Scope Limitation
Request the minimum required permissions (scopes). Do not ask for "everything."
Reduces the platform's security concern and speeds up review.
Support URL
Ensure your support email and URL are active and monitored.
Platforms will test your support responsiveness.
