### Account Information
- Email address

### Content You Create
- Videos you upload or create
- Captions and descriptions

### Automatically Collected Information
- Usage data (how you use our platform)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our service
- Post content to your TikTok account (when authorized)
- Manage your subscription and billing
- Send service-related notifications
- Analyze platform usage to improve features
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account:
- We request permission to post content on your behalf
- We store your TikTok authorization token securely
- We only post content you explicitly create or schedule
- We never access your private TikTok data without permission
- You can revoke access anytime through TikTok settings

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing
- Post to your TikTok account without your explicit action

We MAY share data with:
- TikTok (when you authorize posting)
- Payment processors (for subscription billing)
- Service providers who help operate our platform
- Law enforcement if required by law

## Data Security

We protect your information using:
- Encryption for sensitive data
- Secure servers and databases
- Regular security audits
- Limited employee access
- Industry-standard security practices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
