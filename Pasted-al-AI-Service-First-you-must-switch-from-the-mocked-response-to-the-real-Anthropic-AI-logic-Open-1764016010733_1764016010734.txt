Step 2: Customize the Persona
Once the real AI logic is enabled, the persona is controlled by the systemPrompt variable inside the generateContent function.
Locate this section (around line 55 after uncommenting):
TypeScript
    const systemPrompt = \`
        You are an AI content creator named ${personaName}.
        Your persona is: "${personaDescription}".
        Your goal is to generate engaging, high-quality social media content for a creator platform.
        The content must be safe, non-explicit, and adhere to all platform guidelines.
        Focus on maintaining the specified tone and persona.
        The output should be a single, well-formatted post. DO NOT include any introductory or concluding remarks.
    \`;
How to Change the Persona:
Modify the systemPrompt: You can edit the text inside the backticks (`) to give the AI more detailed and specific instructions.
Focus on the personaDescription: The most effective way to change the persona is to provide a very detailed description in the personaDescription field when you call the API.
Example of a Detailed personaDescription:
Instead of a simple description, you can make it highly specific:
"You are 'Vixen,' a 24-year-old, fiercely independent, and slightly rebellious motorcycle enthusiast from Los Angeles. Your posts are always short, witty, and use a lot of emojis. You speak with a confident, 'I don't care what anyone thinks' attitude. You only post about your bike, your tattoos, and your late-night adventures. You never use formal language."
The AI model will use this detailed instruction to generate content that matches the persona's background, interests, and speaking style.
In summary: The power is in the API call. The more detail you put into the personaDescription field of your API request, the more accurately the AI will emulate the character you want.
Remember to rebuild the project (npm run build) after making any changes to the AIService.ts file.
